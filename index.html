Optimax Energy Systems
Optimax has manufactured HVAC sensors and transmitters for over 30 years for building and energy management systems. Our expertise with sensory type devices and custom design engineering capability has complimented many original equipment manufacturers for building automation and direct digital control. Distributors, contractors, and facility engineers have trust in the Optimax brand delivering quality assured and competitive priced product to their commercial and light industrial projects.

A proud Bemsiq Group company since 2021

Optimax is one of North America’s largest ISO registered manufacturers of HVAC sensors and transmitters.
